ABOUT US

Our Story

Pitch Pro was born on the football pitches of Nigeria. Our founder, a former athlete, saw a massive gap: raw talent in Africa wasn't being discovered because scouting was expensive and lacked data. We created Pitch Pro to bridge that gap. Our platform uses advanced AI and hyper-localized data to streamline the process, democratizing scouting by giving everyone the chance to find the future of football.

Our Mission

To personalize football scouting by building the most accessible and comprehensive platform for discovering emerging talent across Africa.

Our Vision

To be the definitive bridge that connects raw, undiscovered African talent with professional clubs globally, ensuring every player with potential has a chance to be seen.
